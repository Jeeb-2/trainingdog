Let us help deepen that relationship between you and your dog!

Our private training program is designed to give you and your dog exclusive one-on-one personalized attention to correct minor unwanted or unsafe behavior(s) along with introducing/teaching basic obedience commands.This program is perfect for owners who wish to heavily be involved in the training process and learn how to analyze their dogâ€™s behaviors. Conducted through a series of private 1-hour long training sessions, rest assured distractions will be minimal, with no wait times to get answers about how to train your furry companion. Training can take place at our training center, in the comfort of your home, or in local public areas. We teach you hands-on techniques specific to your goals and the unique needs of your dog.

Day Training

Our most popular training program, enjoy a peace of mind while your fur-ever friend spends a fun-filled 8 hours training with us. Great program for puppies to build a strong foundation that sets them up for big time success. This program addresses a range of mild to severe behavior issues depending on program choice and is perfect for owners with less time to commit to the upfront work! Your dog will be trained by our team of professional trainers during the day. In an environment that provides everyday distractions, play time, socialization and attention. Have you always wanted that dog with good manners? We have got you covered. You will get to be present after each training session at the end of each day. At pick up, our trainers will teach you exactly what your dog learned that day. We will provide a hands-on demonstration and give you adequate time to practice your dogs new skills with our trainers, giving you the tools to comfortably move forward with your happy, well mannered companion.

Behaviors addressed in our day train programs include, but are not limited to: mild or severe leash pulling, excessive barking, resource guarding aggression (dog owner, objects or food), leash reactivity, dog/people aggression, dog not coming when called, fearful behavior, dominating behavior, counter surfing, jumping, dog crate aggression, poor listening, rushing the doorway, and/or over excitement

  • Good for puppies, young adults and older dogs who either need a jump start to training or a basic/advanced obedience foundation built. Great program to meet behavioral needs as well.
  • Frequency: 2-3 days a week over the course of 3 or 5 weeks
  • Investment: $2775+

Included in the program:

  • Training update videos available via our Facebook and Instagram
  • Follow up aftercare refresher training after completion to ensure quality of training
  • Phone support during and after program
  • Access to group training and walking sessions
  • 2 year free training support

Day Train Programs

Option 1:

6 days of training (2 days a week for 3 weeks)--No aftercare refreshers*
Commands Taught: Introduction to sit, down, come, leash skills, and wait (doorways and dog crates). Good for puppy training and young dogs who have minimal behavior issues. Great jump start to training

Option 2:

9 days of training (3 days a week for 3 weeks)-- 1 after care refresher*
Commands taught: sit, down, stay, place, come, heel, wait (doorways and dog crates), auto sit (your dog automatically sits when you come to a halt). Good for puppies in need of a solid foundation, dogs who have mild behavioral issues, or dogs who need a solid obedience foundation

Option 3:

15 training days (3 days a week for 5 weeks)--2 aftercare refreshers* Our most advanced day training program. Includes public and distraction training to provide the very best, and high level foundation.
Commands taught: same as Option 2. Good Great for dogs who express severe reactivity or aggression. This includes any pushy and unruly behavior. Young and older dogs are also welcome.
